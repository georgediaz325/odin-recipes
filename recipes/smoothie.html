Green Smoothie

Description

This recipe provides one serving of green smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup stemmed kale
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 1 sliced frozen banana
  • ½ green apple, cut into chunks
  • ½ pear, cut into chunks
  • ¼ cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
  • 1 tablespoon wheat germ
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Blend kale, milk, banana, apple, pear, oats, peanut butter, flax seed meal, wheat germ, and parsley in a blender until smooth.
